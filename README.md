## 📌 Overview

**Eunoia** is an AI-powered 24/7 mental wellness platform built specifically for college students in India. It provides anonymous emotional support, CBT-guided AI conversations, peer circles, and mood tracking — with direct campus counselor integration.

> **"1 in 4 Indian college students faces anxiety or depression. Fewer than 10% ever seek help."**
> Eunoia bridges that gap — anonymously, affordably, and always.

## 🚨 Problem Statement

College students in India lack accessible, anonymous, and affordable mental health support — leading to untreated anxiety, depression, and burnout due to:

- **Stigma** around seeking help
- **Understaffed counseling centers** (1 counselor per 500 students)
- **Zero 24/7 digital alternatives** built for Indian campus culture
- **3-week average wait time** at campus counseling centers

---

## 💡 Solution

Eunoia is a mobile-first platform offering:

- 🤖 **AI Companion** — Empathetic chatbot trained on CBT & DBT frameworks, available 24/7, fully anonymous
- 📊 **Mood Tracker** — Daily check-ins, weekly mood graphs, and smart escalation alerts to campus counselors
- 🌐 **Peer Circles** — Anonymous moderated group chats by topic (exam stress, loneliness, placement anxiety, burnout)
- 🔐 **Zero Identity Login** — No name, no email, no phone number required

| Feature | Description |

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### Frontend
- React Native (cross-platform iOS + Android)
- Tailwind CSS + NativeWind
- Expo (rapid prototyping)

### Backend
- Node.js + Express.js
- MongoDB (user data & mood logs)
- Firebase Auth (anonymous sign-in)

### AI / ML
- Gemini API (conversational AI companion)
- Sentiment Analysis via HuggingFace
- LangChain (CBT-guided conversation flow)

### Infrastructure
- Vercel / Render (deployment)
- Socket.io (real-time peer chat)
- Figma (UI/UX design & prototyping)

## 🎯 Target Users

- Engineering & professional college students (18–25)

- First-year students away from home for the first time
- Students hesitant to visit on-campus counselors due to stigma

---

## ⚔️ Competition & Differentiation

| Competitor | Gap |
|------------|-----|
| Wysa / Woebot | Global focus, no India context, not campus-specific |
| YourDOST | Paid therapist model, not AI-first, costly for students |
| iCall (TISS) | Phone-based, no mobile app, limited reach |
| ChatGPT (informal) | Not purpose-built for mental health, no escalation |

**Why Eunoia wins:**
- 🇮🇳 India-first: regional languages, local cultural nuances, Indian college stressors
- 🏫 Campus integration: direct API to counselor dashboards with smart escalation
- 🆓 Anonymous & free at core: no stigma barrier, no paywall for basic support
